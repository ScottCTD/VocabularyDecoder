External VD Lists

You can make you own VD Lists and memorize your own vocabularies!

Go to the "tools" menu at the top of the windows, and then you can choose the way you want to make your own lists.

If everything goes right, you will get a file with extension ".vd".

Then, you should hit the "import" button on the right side to make your list appear in the file tree.

If you have any question, please firstly check the official instruction in the GitHub page.
